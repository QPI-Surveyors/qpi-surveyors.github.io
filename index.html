Our Story

With over 20 years of combined experience in the surveying industry, QPI Surveyors was founded on the principles of quality, precision, and integrity. Specialising in a wide range of services, we aim to prevent disputes, reduce risks, and streamline on-site tasks for residential clients. Our dedicated team delivers accurate results and clear, actionable insights, making QPI Surveyors a trusted partner in UK construction.

Our Services

  • Schedule of Condition Surveys: We provide detailed assessments of the current condition of properties to help protect your investments.
  • Party Wall Surveys: Ensuring compliance with regulations and offering expert advice to resolve any disputes between adjoining property owners.
  • Measurement Surveys: Providing precise measurements for construction and planning purposes, tailored to your project's needs.
  • CAD Drawings: Offering high-quality CAD drawings for design, planning, and development projects.
